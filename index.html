About關於 A8

A8 Recording is a New York production label and songwriting house working across pop, R&B, and alternative music. We pair full studio production — engineering, vocal production, mixing, and mastering — with original songwriting and lyric composition built for international streaming, radio, and screen. Rooted in conservatory training in composition and jazz and modern music technology from The New School and NYU, A8 moves between commercial hooks and experimental, artist-defining records — with current work extending into interactive and narrative scoring for games.

A8 Recording 始於紐約的音樂製作廠牌與詞曲創作工作室，作品橫跨流行、R&B 與另類音樂。我們將完整的錄音室製作 — 工程、人聲製作、混音與母帶 — 結合原創詞曲與歌詞創作，專為國際串流、廣播與影視市場打造。團隊根植於 The New School 與 NYU 的古典作曲、爵士與音樂科技訓練，作品在商業旋律與實驗性、定義藝人風格的創作之間自由游走，並持續拓展至遊戲互動與敘事配樂領域。
